St. Louis Blues

2019 Stanley Cup Champions

/*Blues Tribute information*/

Here is a timeline of the St. Louis Blues' season journey to the cup:

  • July 1st, 2018 - Blues trade Patrik Berglund, Vladimir Sobotka, Tage Thompson, and a 2nd round pick to Sabres in exchange for Ryan O' Reilly
  • November 19th, 2018 - Blues fire Head Coach Mike Yeo and name Craig Berube Interim Head Coach
  • January 3rd, 2019 - Blues sit at dead last in the league roughly halfway through the season, with a 15-18-4 record
  • January 5th, 2019 - Rookie goaltender Jordan Binnington is called up from AHL affiliate
  • March 29th, 2019 - On the back of strong performances from Binnington and company, Blues go 30-10-5 to close out regular season and earn playoff birth
  • June 12th, 2019 - Saint Louis Blues defeat Boston Bruins in 7 games to clinch first ever franchise Stanley Cup
